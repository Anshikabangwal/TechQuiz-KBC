Welcome to the TechQuiz KBC!

Before you begin, please read the following instructions carefully to understand the rules and how to make the best use of the available lifelines.

Quiz Overview:

  • Total Questions: 14
  • Answer Options: Each question has 4 options, but only one is the correct answer.
  • Timer: You have 60 seconds to answer each question. If the timer runs out, the quiz will end.

Lifelines:

You have three lifelines to help you during the quiz. Each lifeline can be used once:

  • 50-50: This lifeline will remove two incorrect options, leaving you with one correct and one incorrect option to choose from.
  • Swap: Use this lifeline to swap the current question for a different one. The swapped question cannot be swapped again.
  • Quit: If you decide to leave the quiz, use this lifeline to quit. Your progress up to that point will be recorded, but the quiz will end.

Important Rules:

  • Incorrect Answer: If you answer any question incorrectly, the quiz will end immediately.
  • Timer Expiry: If you do not answer a question within the 60-second time limit, the quiz will end.

Strategy Tips:

  • Use the 50-50 lifeline when you are unsure between multiple options.
  • Choose the Swap lifeline if you find a question particularly challenging or outside your knowledge area.
  • Use the Quit lifeline if you wish to end the quiz and secure your current progress without risking an incorrect answer.

Good luck, and enjoy the challenge!
